Meta Automation

We engineer advanced lead-capture frameworks and automated communication workflows integrated with Meta's enterprise Graph API across WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebook.

By connecting real-time comment-to-DM triggers, automatic database syncing, and predictive routing, we eradicate commercial conversion friction and scale message delivery.

What We Offer

  • API-Driven Conversation Logic: Programmable conversational pipelines across WhatsApp, Instagram, and Messenger for immediate client engagement.
  • High-Speed Lead Qualification: Automating the ingestion, verification, and scoring of commercial inquiries before they reach your sales desk.
  • CRM & Data Integrations: Real-time lead syncing from Meta APIs directly into Salesforce, HubSpot, or custom database platforms.
  • Targeted Ad Event Automation: Configuring conversion API (CAPI) webhooks to pass precise post-interaction events back to ad networks for ROAS optimization.

Who This Is For

This integration is engineered for fast-scaling e-commerce enterprises, high-volume consumer services, and B2B organizations processing substantial customer traffic. It is built for teams looking to reclaim hours of manual lead triage and eliminate customer drop-offs caused by delayed response times. If you seek to deploy automated customer journeys, qualify leads at scale, and maximize advertising conversion rates without expanding support headcount, this system is for you.

Our Process

  1. Pipeline Architecture & Mapping: Visualizing the ideal customer journey, data capture points, and integration requirements.
  2. Graph API Implementation & Scripting: Building custom webhooks and deploying API communication logic in compliance with platform guidelines.
  3. Workflow Automation & Syncing: Linking conversational systems to your internal databases and CRM pipelines for instant synchronization.
  4. Friction Auditing & Iteration: Running automated tests to identify conversion blockages, optimizing phrasing, and refining bot-to-agent transitions.

Expected Outcomes

  • Zero-Latency Inbound Triage: Shrinking lead response times to under 3 seconds, significantly boosting customer acquisition metrics.
  • Programmatic Lead Qualification: Pre-qualifying up to 75% of inbound inquiries through automated logical branch flows.
  • Frictionless Funnel Scaling: Managing thousands of simultaneous customer interactions without operational bottlenecks.
